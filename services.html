Our Pediatric Services

Comprehensive, compassionate care for every child — from newborns to adolescents, delivered with clinical excellence.

Complete Care

Specialized Services for Young Patients

Dr. Atul Krishna offers a comprehensive range of pediatric medical services, leveraging advanced training at one of India's premier hospitals.

Newborn & Neonatal Care

Expert, round-the-clock management of newborns in the NICU. Services include assessment of newborn health, management of premature babies, CPAP therapy, surfactant administration, exchange transfusions, and attendance at normal and high-risk deliveries. Dr. Atul is trained to handle the most complex neonatal cases.

Vaccination & Immunization

Complete immunization services following the latest IAP (Indian Academy of Pediatrics) and WHO guidelines. Includes routine childhood vaccines, catch-up schedules, travel vaccines, and counseling for parents on the importance of timely immunization to protect children from preventable diseases.

Growth & Developmental Assessment

Systematic monitoring of weight, height, head circumference, and developmental milestones across physical, cognitive, and behavioral domains. Early identification and intervention for developmental delays, learning difficulties, and behavioral challenges to ensure every child reaches their full potential.

Pediatric Emergency Care

Rapid response to pediatric emergencies including respiratory distress, severe allergic reactions, status epilepticus, high fevers, dehydration, and trauma. Dr. Atul is trained in PALS (Pediatric Advanced Life Support) and experienced in stabilizing critically ill children in emergency scenarios.

Pediatric Intensive Care (PICU)

Management of critically ill children requiring intensive monitoring and life support. Services include mechanical ventilation, hemodynamic monitoring, management of multi-organ failure, and post-operative critical care. Expert handling of pediatric sepsis, encephalitis, and severe pneumonia.

General Pediatric Consultation

Comprehensive well-child visits and management of common childhood illnesses including respiratory infections, gastroenteritis, fever, skin conditions, and nutritional issues. Regular health assessments with personalized guidance on feeding, sleep, and development for parents at every stage.

Infectious Disease Management

Diagnosis and management of childhood infectious diseases including meningitis, encephalitis, tuberculosis, typhoid, dengue, malaria, and COVID-19. Experience in pediatric HIV/AIDS management and immunocompromised patients from the super-specialty units at RML Hospital.

Pediatric Hematology & Oncology

Support and management of pediatric blood disorders, anemia, thalassemia, and oncology cases. Skilled in performing bone marrow biopsies and aspirations for accurate diagnosis of hematological conditions, along with interpretation of results for tailored treatment planning.
