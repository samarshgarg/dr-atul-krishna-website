About the Doctor

A Dedicated Advocate for Children's Health

Dr. Atul Krishna is a highly trained Paediatric Specialist with an MD in Paediatrics from ABVIMS & Dr. RML Hospital, New Delhi, affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. His passion lies in neonatology and ensuring every child gets the best possible start in life.

  • MD Paediatrics – cleared in First Attempt (2020), GGSIPU, New Delhi
  • Senior Resident in Neonatology at ABVIMS & Dr. RML Hospital
  • Expert in NICU, PICU, Neonatal Resuscitation & Intubation
  • President of RDA RML Hospital (2020–2021) & Vice President, FORDA
  • Certified in Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)
  • Researcher & Published Author in Pediatric Medicine

What We Offer

Comprehensive Pediatric Services

From newborn care to adolescent health, Dr. Atul Krishna offers a wide spectrum of specialized pediatric services with the highest standards of medical care.

Newborn & Neonatal Care

Expert care for newborns including NICU management, surfactant therapy, exchange transfusion, and high-risk delivery attendance.

Vaccination & Immunization

Comprehensive immunization schedules following IAP and WHO guidelines to protect your child from preventable diseases.

Growth & Development

Regular monitoring of physical, cognitive, and developmental milestones to ensure your child is thriving at every stage.

Pediatric Emergency Care

Immediate response to respiratory distress, seizures, high fevers, and other pediatric emergencies with expert ICU-level support.

Pediatric ICU (PICU)

Intensive monitoring and treatment for critically ill children, including central line placement, intubation, and complex procedures.

Diagnostic & Specialist Procedures

Advanced diagnostic skills including bone marrow biopsy, lumbar puncture, liver/kidney biopsy, and ICD insertion for accurate diagnosis.
